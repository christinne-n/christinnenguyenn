Discord Figma Redesign

For my Human-Computer Interaction class, my group and I redesigned the Discord app in Figma to create a cleaner, more intuitive, and accessible interface. we used Norman’s Principles, the Eight Golden Rules, WCAG guidelines, and Gestalt principles to make improvements to the overlay. The propject process included user research, expert reviews, wireframing, high-fidelity mockups, and an interactive prototype. The final design streamlines navigation, simplifies the layout, and enhances usability for both new and returning users. This project strengthened my skills in user-centered design, design critique, and Figma prototyping, while demonstrating how research-driven design decisions can significantly improve user experience for both new and returning users.

Note: For the prototype, please press Shift + `(backtick) for the overlay to appear.

Sephora Figma Redesign & Prototype

I created a mobile and desktop redesign of Sephora in Figma to practice basic interactions, controls, and components. I tried focusing on simplifying the app to highlight deals, store locations, and featured products while reducing clutter and confusing navigation. I wanted to practice creating a simple prototype that would allow users to easily navigate through the app and find what they were looking for. For the desktop version, I aimed to simplify the Sephora home page by removing unnecessary sections, emphasizing main categories, and making browsing faster without excessive scrolling.
