About

With a background in biology and a strong interest in healthcare, my goal was to make a difference in people’s lives. Along the way, I discovered how powerful and innovative technology can be in solving real-world problems more efficiently, as it led me to pursue software engineering at UCI. I’m currently learning full-stack web development through the OdinProject and building tools that are both meaningful and easy to use as I continue my education in this field.

I enjoy developing projects and solutions that combine both technical and functional efficiency. I hope to create software that not only meets user needs, but also enhances their experience. As I continue with my journey, I’m excited to grow beyond web development and eventually develop innovative software across broader platforms in other areas of both technology and healthcare.

Discord Figma Redesign

Redesigned Discord overlay in Figma to create a cleaner and accessible interface. Utilized Norman’s Principles, the Eight Golden Rules, WCAG guidelines, and Gestalt principles. Overall project process included user research, expert reviews, wireframing, high-fidelity mockups, and an interactive prototype. Final design streamlines navigation, simplifies the layout, and enhances usability for both new and returning users.

Note: For the prototype, please press Shift + `(backtick) for the overlay to appear.
