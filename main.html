About

With a background in biology and a strong interest in healthcare, my goal was to make a difference in people’s lives. Along the way, I discovered how powerful and innovative technology can be in solving real-world problems more efficiently, as it led me to pursue software engineering at UCI.

I’m currently focusing on full-stack web development and building tools that are both meaningful and easy to use as I continue my education in this field. I'm proficient in C++, Java, Python, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I have experience using Figma, Git and Github, as I have taken classes related to Data Structures, Human Computer Interaction, and SQL in the upcoming fall quarter at UCI.

I enjoy developing projects and solutions that combine both technical and functional efficiency. I hope to create software that not only meets user needs, but also enhances their experience. As I continue with my journey, I’m excited to grow beyond web development and eventually develop innovative software across broader platforms in other areas of both technology and healthcare.
