About

With a background in biology and a strong interest in healthcare, I wanted to make a difference in people’s lives. Along the way, I discovered how powerful and innovative software can be in solving real-world problems more efficiently, as it led me to pursue software engineering at UCI. I’m currently focusing on full-stack web development and building tools that are both meaningful and easy to use as I continue my education in this field. I’m excited to grow beyond web development and eventually develop helpful and innovative software across broader platforms in other areas of technology and healthcare.
